About Liam Designs

Custom, hand coded websites for real businesses

I build fast, clean and fully custom websites using hand written HTML, CSS and JavaScript. No WordPress, no page builders, no pre made templates. Every project is coded from scratch to match your brand, your content and the goals of your business.

I focus on clear structure, easy navigation and responsive layouts that work perfectly on mobiles, tablets and desktops.
